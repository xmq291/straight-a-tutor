Q: How to search for a tutor?

Answer

On our website enter the subject you want to learn, your ZIP code, and click Get Started. Answer a few questions that will help us list qualified tutors for you. Find a tutor who meet your needs. You can message tutors from their profiles. To message a tutor, click the link and enter your first name, last name, and email address. We'll also ask how you found out about Wyzant, and give you the option to have other tutors contact you about your request. Accept our Terms of Use to send your message to the tutor you selected. We'll email you a link to create a password for your account.
